Why you should vote

The primary season is currently commenced with the general election coming in only a matter of months. The national and local leaders chosen will have a significant effect on many important issues. Learn why it's more important than ever that you make your way to vote at the polls.

Make Your Voice Heard

It is your right to vote and we have a duty to excersize this right. Around the world, many goverments exclude their citizens from elections and we have the opportunity to help chose the future leaders.

Every Vote Counts

Voting is one of the most significant privileges and responsibilities we have as U.S citizens! If you are passionate about a particular social issue, voting is a way that you can help improve the social agenda in the United States. Learn about candidates' views on social issues that matter to you and support the best canidate that will make the best change.

The Importance of Voting

Many people have the false belief that their vote will not make a difference however it is proven that elections can be decided by a handful of votes. Many local contests are determined by a flip of the coin. By taking the initiative to vote, you are preventing the election to by left to chance.

Politicans Will Not Recognize Your Needs Without Your Vote

Every canidate knows who will vote in each election and they tend to support those who are apart of the higher voter turnout. You have a part to help represent the communities you are apart of by turning out in greater numbers than is traditionally seen.
